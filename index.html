About Us

OUR GROUP MOTTO - "UNITED WE STAND .. UNITED WE RIDE"

Biking is not a hobby its a passion and we add adventure and wildness to it so that we come home with a proud feeling of a true biker and brotherhood. This group is related to adventure and spreading bikerhood through creating events in a proper way and spreading immense love in the society.

We are not concerned with any make of bikes and cc of bikes anyone can join us who want to be a part of our rides and events. For our events do like our page to get regular updates & details.
