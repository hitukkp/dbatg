About Us

OUR GROUP MOTTO - "UNITED WE STAND .. UNITED WE RIDE"

Biking is not a hobby its a passion and we add adventure and wildness to it so that we come home with a proud feeling of a true biker and brotherhood. This group is related to adventure and spreading bikerhood through creating events in a proper way and spreading immense love in the society.

We are not concerned with any make of bikes and cc of bikes anyone can join us who want to be a part of our rides and events. For our events do like our page to get regular updates & details.

BIKERS VS WILD :- We have started first ever outing BIKERS VS WILD series in which we will go to new places and explore those places by doing adventure activities, camping, bonefire etc. so prepare yourself to become a part of this unique series. Bikers vs wild gives you an oppourtunity to explore yourself and provide you an experience which you have always dreamt about. Meet new riders and share experience and while going back you get a feeling of proud. Delhi biker Adventure tour group provides you an oppourtunity to be what you want to do ...explore places which are undiscovered and be a part of new revolution in biking world...
